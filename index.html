Welcome

Dronomous is an autonomous drone and robotic technology development startup based in Melbourne, Australia that provides solutions, services, and platform for operations which require less human intervention.

Cutting edge technology

We make beautiful & functional multi purpose drones

Who are we?

The founding members of the startup are from Monash University, Melbourne, Australia with various specialization in the fields of robotics, computer science, material science, and mechanical engineering.

What we provide

We develop and promote drone and robotic technology in the field of various sectors such as sanitisation and cleaning industry, logistics, agriculture and food processing industry, surveillance and monitoring industry, etc.

Technology at its finest

The challenge

Cleaning and disinfecting high-touch surfaces at public places/hospitals to prevent the spread of COVID-19 or similar infectious diseases.
Surveillance and monitoring of public places along with cleaning if required.

The solution

Our tech startup will develop autonomous drones and robots that can perform contactless disinfection of a contagious area.
It can monitor and perform surveillance of the contagious area.

  • Autonomous drones
  • Contactless disinfection
  • Surveillance of the contagious area

Also..

Our company can provide services such as contactless autonomous cleaning, surveillance, logistics according to the client's requirements.

Our Products

The first drone designed by Dronomous, Kite1, is an autonomous drone that can be extensively used in cleaning hospitals and aged care houses in the present pandemic situation. The drone has inbuilt UVC lights and disinfectants. The disinfectant is sprayed as the drone navigates the room.

Services

Dronomous also provides a wide range of services apart from the products it offers. Services include renting the multipurpose drones, disinfecting services to name a few.

Deep disinfecting services

Our approved autonomous drone will disinfect all surfaces and areas with high touch – workstations, floors, kitchens, toilets. We provide this service for hospitals, offices, aged care centres, etc.

Training

We provide training on a range of technology topics that includes the assembly of hobby drones, understanding the autonomous drone working principles.

Workshops

We provide workshops on a range of technology topics that includes 3D printing of drone frames, material selection criteria, simulation of flying objects

Online courses

We provide courses on a range of technology topics that includes understanding the autonomous drone working principles, 3D printing of drone frames, simulation of flying objects etc..

Custom drones and accessories

We will provide a complete solution right from design, material, and components selection to building a custom drone as per customer's specific requirements.

Rent our products

We provide a rental service where you can rent any of our existing products for a particular amount of time. This can be further discussed in detail with our team.
